Technology Workshops for Kids

MINT Lab: Berlin is a hands-on educational program designed to engage children of all ages in the exciting and in demand world of MINT. Since we were founded in Berlin, we continue to use the MINT acronym, which is the German equivalent to STEM in the English speaking world.
